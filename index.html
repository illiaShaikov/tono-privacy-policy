Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Tono: White Noise & Sleep Calm (package name com.tono.baby.sleep), published by Illia Shaikov ("Tono", "we", "our", or "us").

Overview

Tono is an offline baby sleep and white-noise app. The app plays audio files that are included inside the app package and lets you save local preferences such as theme, language, sound volumes, saved mixes, and timer settings.

We do not show ads, do not use advertising SDKs, do not use the Android Advertising ID, and do not sell personal data. We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app reliability and basic product usage so we can improve Tono after release.

Data We Collect

Tono does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, microphone, camera, or photos.

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • selected theme and language;
  • default sound volume;
  • saved mixes and per-sound volume settings;
  • sleep timer and delayed start settings;
  • whether the first-run safety prompt has been acknowledged.

This local information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Tono uses Firebase Analytics to collect basic app usage data, such as app opens, sessions, screen views, selected sound IDs, timer actions, saved mix actions, app version, device model, operating system version, language, approximate region, and app instance identifiers. These events are used to understand which features work well and which parts of the app need improvement.

Tono uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash and diagnostic data, such as crash stack traces, non-fatal error reports, app version, operating system version, device model, timestamps, and diagnostic identifiers. This helps us find and fix crashes after a release.

Firebase Analytics is configured without Android Advertising ID collection and without ad personalization signals.

Permissions

Tono may request or use the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications: used to show media playback controls and background audio status on supported Android versions.
  • Foreground service / media playback: used to keep audio playback stable when the screen is off or the app is in the background.
  • Wake lock: used only as needed by the audio playback service to help keep audio running reliably.

These permissions are used for app functionality and are not used to show advertising or collect the Android Advertising ID.

Third-Party Services

Tono may use platform services such as Google Play's in-app review flow, app store links, and the system share sheet when you choose those actions. These services are provided by the operating system or app store platform.

Tono uses Google Firebase services for analytics and crash reporting:

  • Firebase privacy and security information
  • Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Tono is intended to be used by parents, guardians, and caregivers. The app does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Firebase analytics and crash diagnostics are used to improve app quality and are not used to identify children or build advertising profiles.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Usage and diagnostic data may be processed by Google Firebase as our service provider for analytics and crash reporting. We do not share data with advertising networks.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local app settings remain on your device until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

If you contact us for support, we will use the information you provide only to respond to your request. You may ask us to delete support messages associated with you by contacting us at the email address below.

Firebase analytics and crash diagnostic data are retained according to our Firebase project settings and Google Firebase's service terms.

Security

Your saved mixes and settings remain on your device. Usage and diagnostic data sent to Firebase is handled by Google Firebase infrastructure. If you contact us by email, that communication is handled by standard email service providers.
